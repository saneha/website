About Us

Remarkit's team has, as its core value, the passion to strive for the perfect project or service. The basic belief is that if you strive for perfection then at the very least you will hit excellence. Our aim to be successful through the provision of innovative solutions that assist our clients with meeting their technology goals.
One of our key strengths is the ability to see tomorrow's possibilities and challenges today, and through an innovative development process, provide the services to meet them. Remarkit have been described by many of its clients as a company that "just doesn't give up", and who "goes the extra mile" with the inspiring attitude that nothing is “too much bother”.
The client centric values we embrace has lead to a professionalism and commitment that has many times exceeded our client’s expectations.

Remarkit have been independently audited by Grant Thornton New Zealand against Information Security Management (NZISM) controls relevant to Remarkit as a provider of IT disposal services to New Zealand Government agencies
